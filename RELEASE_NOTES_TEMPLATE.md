# Release Notes Template

Copy this into Play Console's "What's new in this version" field (max 500
characters) for each release. Keep entries short, user-facing, and specific
— skip internal refactors (like a DI framework change) unless they visibly
affect the user.

---

## v[VERSION] ([DATE])

**New**
- [Feature]

**Improved**
- [Improvement]

**Fixed**
- [Bug fix]

---

## Example

## v1.1 (2026-08-01)

**New**
- Dark mode toggle in the top bar.

**Improved**
- Faster QR scanning in low light.

**Fixed**
- Fixed a crash when scanning a malformed WiFi QR code.

---

### Internal changelog (not for Play Store — keep in your own release doc / CHANGELOG.md if you want full history)

Use this section (or a separate `CHANGELOG.md`) to track everything,
including non-user-facing changes like dependency bumps, DI refactors, or CI
changes, for your own team's reference.
